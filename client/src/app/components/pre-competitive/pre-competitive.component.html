Pre-Competitive Program



Edge offers several Pre-Competitive tracks to meet the age, ability level, commitment level, and goals of our gymnasts, including the Development Program (“Devo”), Girls Pre-Team, and Boys Pre-Team. All of our Pre-Competitive programs are by invitation only.

Gymnasts participating in a Pre-Competitive Program must enroll in the program year round, in order to meet the expectations set in the training program.

Commitment is required from the athletes, parents, and coaches in order to achieve the goals set for the athletes and to ensure a positive gymnastics experience for all involved. The coaches’ goal at each level is to give each athlete the tools and coaching to allow them, with dedication on their part, to reach their potential. In selecting athletes for the program, the coaches must have confidence that each child has the potential to compete successfully. It is then the coaches’ and athletes’ responsibility to prepare for competitive success. The coach will decide when the athlete is ready for a competitive season.

Girls Developmental 1: ages 4-5 / practices 2 ½ hours per week
Girls Developmental 2: ages 5-6 / practices 4 ½ hours per week
Girls Developmental 3: ages 6-7 / practices 6 hours per week

Girls Pre-Team: ages 9 and up / practices 6 hours per week

Boys Pre-Team: 6 and up / practices 6 hours per week
