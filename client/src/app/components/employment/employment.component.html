Gymnastics Coach – Academy / Recreation


Edge Gymnastics Training Center – Immediate Opening

Edge seeks enthusiastic teachers, and coaches, to contribute their experience, creativity, and energy to provide the ultimate gymnastics experience.

We are currently looking for coaches at all levels of our gymnastics program including:
Preschool Classes - The Pre-school Gymnastics Program is designed to teach youngsters gymnastics in a safe, entertaining, and structured environment.

Academy Classes - The Academy Gymnastics Program is a progression-based program for girls and boys designed in accordance with the standards of USA Gymnastics.

Tumbling Classes - Tumbling classes are available for students who enjoy flying through the air, rolling, and flipping on the floor.

Ideal candidate will have:
· A passion for working with children
· Experience working with children a plus
· Enthusiastic approach to learning and teaching new skills
· Ability to establish and maintain good inter-personal relationships with parents, children, staff and the community
· Excellent communication skills
· Organized approach to record keeping
· Gymnastics experience is a plus, but not required. We will train you!
