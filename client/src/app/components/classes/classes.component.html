Preschool Classes


For ages 12 months to 5 years, the Preschool Gymnastics Program at Edge is designed to teach our young students gymnastics in a safe, entertaining, and structured environment. Our goal is to teach preschoolers developmentally appropriate gymnastics activities as they also work on building confidence, socializing with other students and teachers, and having fun! The appropriate class for your child is determined by age. Within the class, the instructors will adjust the skills to challenge each child.

The class levels, by age, are as follows:

Gym Explorers:

Gym Explorers is a 30 minute class. This class includes parachute, circle time, exploring stations and obstacle courses, hand-eye activities, trampoline and foam pit.

Kindergym:

Kindergym is a 45 minute class. This class begins with warm-ups and stretching. Then students participate in event circuits (obstacle courses), including floor, vault, balance beam, and bar skills. Students and parents enjoy quality time together while working on body awareness, basic tumbling skills, balancing, swinging, hand-eye coordination, and more!

Mini-Mites:

Mini-Mites is a 45 minute class. Students begin with a 10 minute cardio and warm-up. The class then participates in two gymnastics events in a circuit format and one hand-eye activity. Students work on basic gymnastics skills while also working on social and school-readiness skills.

Mighty-Mites:

Mighty-Mites is a 45 minute class. Students begin with a 10 minute cardio and warm-up. Students participate in two gymnastics events in a circuit format and one hand-eye activity. Students work on basic gymnastics skills, progressing to a higher level than the Mini-Mites class.

Dynamites:

Dynamites is a 45 minute class. It is a transition class between our Preschool Program and our Academy Program. Students work on perfecting their basic gymnastics skills, while working on strength, flexibility, balance, coordination, confidence and more!

Preschool classes are strictly age-based. Students wishing to enroll in a Preschool class must be the required minimum age on the first day of class.



Girls’ Academy


For ages 6 to 17 years, the Girls’ Academy at Edge is a progressive program based on the standards set forth by USA Gymnastics. Each week, students will work towards mastering a set of predetermined skills in each of the four women’s gymnastics events: Vault, Uneven Bars, Beam and Floor.

The Girls’ Academy is divided into 5 levels: Beginner, Advanced Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced Intermediate and Advanced. All students must start in Beginner, regardless of age, unless evaluated and approved for a higher level by one of our senior Instructors.

Students wishing to enroll in the Girls’ Academy must be the required minimum age (6 years old) on the first day of class.


Boys’ Academy


For ages 6 to 17 years, the Boys’ Academy at Edge is a progressive program based on the standards set forth by USA Gymnastics. Each week, students will work towards mastering a set of predetermined skills in each of the six men’s gymnastics events: Vault, Parallel Bars, Hi-Bar, Rings, Pommel and Floor.

The Boys’ Academy is divided into 3 levels: Beginner, Advanced Beginner, and Intermediate. All students must start in Beginner, regardless of age, unless evaluated and approved for a higher level by one of our senior Instructors.

Students wishing to enroll in the Boys’ Academy must be the required minimum age (6 years old) on the first day of class.


Tumbling Classes


For boys and girls ages 6 to 17, our Tumbling classes are progression-based, starting with basic handstands, bridges and cartwheels. Our highest level class (Power Tumbling) works on connected passes, like round-off, back handsprings, and round-off, back tucks.

Tumbling is divided into 5 levels: Beginner, Advanced Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced and Power Tumbling. All students must start in Beginner, regardless of age, unless evaluated and approved for a higher level by one of our senior Instructors.

Students wishing to enroll in Tumbling classes must be the required minimum age (6 years old) on the first day of class.

CLASS MAKE-UP POLICY: Students can make-up one (1) class per month in Open Gym. The total number of make-ups allowed in a calendar year is capped at six (6). Make-ups must be completed within 30 days of the missed class. No refunds or credits will be given for missed classes.
