Classes


Preschool Classes
The Pre-school Gymnastics Program at Edge is designed to teach youngsters gymnastics in a safe, entertaining and structured environment.

Academy Classes
The Academy Gymnastics Program at Edge is a progression-based program for girls and boys designed in accordance with the standards of USA Gymnastics.

Tumbling Classes
Edge offers Tramp-N-Tumble for students who enjoy flying through the air and rolling and flipping on the floor and also specialized Tumbling for Cheerleading classes.


Squad Tumbling Classes
Specialized Squad tumbling classes for Cheerleading teams are available at Edge!

Development Classes
Edge offers Development gymnastics classes for those young gymnasts who show exceptional potential for gymnastics.

CLASS MAKE-UP POLICY: For session classes (Preschool, Academy & Tumbling), we offer 2 make-ups per session for missed classes in Open Gym. This does not apply to City of Dublin classes or monthly classes.
